What Bearded Dragons Need

Required Supplies Purpose
Enclosure or Terrarium Make sure it is big enough to house your dragon
UV lighting Ensures your dragon recieves proper nutrients
Basking/Heating Lamp Keeps the enclosure at the correct temperature range
Thermometer Helps keep track of the temperature/humidity
Substrate Cleanable surface for your dragon to walk on
Food and Water Dishes So your dragon doesn't starve or get thirsty
Tank decor Provides multiple places for your dragon to sit, bask, hide
Calcium Powder To sprinkle on their food to provide essential nutrients
Worms, Crickets, Roaches etc. Especially at a young age, beardies need lots of protein
Flat Rock Helps trap heat and provides a place to bask
